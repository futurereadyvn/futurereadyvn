Tan Hung Church — Piano

Started: 2024 · Weekly · Group class + practice logs

Beginning the Journey

The project began with simple hopes — to bring music into the lives of children who never thought they could touch an instrument. With four small keyboards and endless laughter, our first class filled the room with curiosity.

Learning to Listen

Each week, the kids learn more than notes. They learn patience, teamwork, and how to listen — not only to rhythm but to each other. They also write reflections in their practice logs, a small diary of progress and joy.

Mini-Recitals

Once every month, the church hall transforms into a tiny concert space. Families gather, volunteers accompany, and little hands play melodies of hope. For us, it’s not just music — it’s confidence growing louder.

“Teaching piano here has shown us that the smallest lessons can open the biggest hearts.”
