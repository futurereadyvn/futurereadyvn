Program — Science

Hands-on science with everyday materials — observe, hypothesize, test, and explain like young scientists.

What we teach

  • Scientific thinking: variables, fair tests, simple data.
  • Core topics: forces, energy, states of matter, ecosystems.
  • Safety & lab hygiene using school-safe substitutes.

Activities

  • Paper rockets, balloon cars, capillary action flowers.
  • Density towers; melting/evaporation mini-labs.
  • Leaf surveys & micro-ecosystem jars.

Outcomes

  • Explain cause-effect using basic models.
  • Record observations clearly; present results.
  • Curiosity for how things work.

Who it’s for

  • Primary and early secondary students.
  • Clubs or weekend workshops at partner sites.
Want to host? We can pack a portable “mini-lab” and come to you.
