Program — Vietnamese

Reading, writing, and speaking with confidence — cultural stories first, grammar follows.

What we teach

  • Reading fluency with short stories & folk tales.
  • Writing basics: sentences → paragraphs.
  • Pronunciation and everyday conversation.

Activities

  • Story circles; read-aloud & role-play.
  • Vocabulary games; picture prompts.
  • Simple journals & thank-you letters.

Outcomes

  • Clearer speech & stronger reading habits.
  • Ability to write short, coherent pieces.
  • Love for Vietnamese stories & culture.

Who it’s for

  • Primary learners; heritage learners needing practice.
We use bilingual supports when helpful and keep lessons practical for home practice.
