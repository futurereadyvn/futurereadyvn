Riley

Vietnamese Mentor — Future Ready Vietnam

BVIS Year 12 student and Vietnamese Mentor at FRV. Riley supports younger learners in building confident, clear Vietnamese through patient guidance, practice structure, and gentle feedback.

Education

  • BVIS — Year 12.
  • Strong Vietnamese literacy; bilingual study environment.

Teaching Focus

  • Reading comprehension & expressive writing (age-appropriate prompts).
  • Pronunciation & fluency through short, fun speaking tasks.
  • Vocabulary ladders and mini-quizzes to track progress.

Experience & Activities

  • Volunteer teaching with FRV’s free community classes.
  • Prepared lesson handouts and practice logs for parents.

Skills

  • Warm, encouraging classroom tone.
  • Clear scaffolding for younger learners.
  • Bilingual explanation when needed.

Personal note

I believe Vietnamese is not only a language but also a bridge to culture, family, and identity. I try to make every session calm, kind, and practical — so students feel safe to speak, write, and grow.
