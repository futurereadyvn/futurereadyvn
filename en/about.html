About FRV

Future Ready Vietnam (FRV) is a student-led, non-profit project that donates knowledge — not money — to young learners. We teach via Music, Computer Science, Civics, Mathematics, Science and English (basic) with a minimalist & practical approach.

FRV Falcon: clarity, speed, purpose, courage through practice.
