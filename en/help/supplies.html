About Us

Future Ready Vietnam (FRV) is a student-led, non-profit initiative — a Charity of Knowledge — that shares learning by teaching. We partner with community spaces and volunteer mentors to offer free classes in Music, Science, Math, Vietnamese, English, and Computer Science.
