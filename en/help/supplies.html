What we can accept

  • Basic stationery: notebooks, pencils, erasers, rulers, crayons.
  • Textbooks/workbooks in good condition (recent editions preferred).
  • Art materials: brushes, watercolors, sketchbooks.
  • Music: spare strings, reeds, metronomes, stands, simple percussion.

Notes

  • Please avoid damaged/illegible books or unsafe items.
  • We’ll follow up by email to coordinate logistics and consent.
  • Receipts (if any) can be noted for our internal transparency report.
