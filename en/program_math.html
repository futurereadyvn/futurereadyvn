Program — Math

Playful problem-solving: patterns, logic, and number sense that stick.

What we teach

  • Number sense, fractions, ratios, percentages.
  • Algebra through patterns & puzzles.
  • Geometry with paper folding & tangrams.

Activities

  • Math games & stations; team challenges.
  • Real-life word problems (budget, time, distance).
  • Starter prep for contests (e.g., UKMT style).

Outcomes

  • Confident arithmetic & flexible strategies.
  • Explain reasoning with simple diagrams.
  • Positive math mindset.

Who it’s for

  • Primary to early secondary.
  • Small groups (6–12) or 1-to-few tutoring.
We welcome volunteer tutors who enjoy puzzles and patient coaching.
